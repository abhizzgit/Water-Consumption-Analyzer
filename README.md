# 💧 Water Consumption Pattern Analyzer

** Project Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing water usage patterns to identify areas with high consumption, especially during the summer season. The goal is to derive actionable insights and suggest effective water conservation strategies.

---
 Problem Statement

Analyze water usage patterns to identify zones with high water consumption during summer and recommend suitable conservation measures.

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset includes:

* Zone-wise water consumption data
* Monthly usage statistics
* Population data
* Sector-wise consumption (residential, commercial, industrial)
* Weather data (temperature, rainfall)

---

## 🛠️ Tasks Performed

### 1. Data Loading

* Imported the dataset into a Jupyter Notebook using Python libraries such as Pandas and NumPy.

### 2. Data Cleaning

* Handled missing values
* Standardized column formats
* Verified data consistency

### 3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Analyzed trends in water consumption across months and zones
* Compared sector-wise usage
* Studied the impact of temperature and rainfall on water demand

### 4. Peak Usage Identification

* Identified high consumption periods, especially during summer (April–June)
* Detected zones with consistently high water usage

### 5. Data Visualization

* Line charts for monthly trends
* Bar charts for zone comparison
* Area charts for sector-wise distribution
* Heatmaps to highlight high consumption areas

### 6. Insights & Recommendations

* Highlighted zones with excessive water usage
* Identified seasonal patterns affecting demand
* Suggested conservation strategies

---

Key Insights

* Water consumption significantly increases during the summer season
* Certain zones consistently show higher usage due to population and commercial activity
* Lower rainfall correlates with higher water demand
* Residential usage dominates overall consumption

---

Conservation Recommendations

* Promote water-saving practices in high-consumption zones
* Encourage rainwater harvesting
* Implement smart water monitoring systems
* Raise public awareness about efficient water usage
